# WTF is SOC 2?

SOC 2 is an auditing standard that verifies a company handles customer data securely. An independent auditor checks your controls against five "Trust Service Criteria": security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy. Enterprise customers often require SOC 2 reports before buying your SaaS product.

```
Trust Service Criteria:

  Security           Is the system protected against unauthorized access?
  Availability       Is the system available as promised (SLA)?
  Processing         Does the system process data correctly?
  Confidentiality    Is confidential data protected?
  Privacy            Is personal data handled per privacy notice?

Type I:   Controls exist at a point in time (snapshot)
Type II:  Controls work over a period (3-12 months)

  Enterprise buyer: "Show me your SOC 2 Type II"
  You: hand over auditor's report
```

**Examples:** [Vanta](https://www.vanta.com/), [Drata](https://drata.com/), [Secureframe](https://secureframe.com/)
