# WTF is GDPR?

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) is the EU law that gives people control over their personal data. If you process data of anyone in the EU, you must get consent, explain what you collect and why, let users access/delete their data, report breaches within 72 hours, and potentially appoint a Data Protection Officer. Fines go up to 4% of global annual revenue.

```
Key rights for users:
  ● Right to access (show me my data)
  ● Right to deletion (delete my data)
  ● Right to portability (export my data)
  ● Right to rectification (fix my data)
  ● Right to object (stop processing my data)

Key obligations for companies:
  ● Lawful basis for processing
  ● Data minimization (collect only what you need)
  ● Breach notification within 72 hours
  ● Privacy by design
  ● Fines: up to 20M EUR or 4% of revenue
```
